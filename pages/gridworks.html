GRIDWORKS

A tiny factory. Every sprite is 5×5 pixels. Mine ore, automate everything, launch a rocket.

THE LOOP

  • Hand-mine: hold left-click on an ore patch. Loot appears in the strip at the top of the screen.
  • Craft (E): turn plates into gears, circuits, and machines by hand.
  • Automate: Miners dig ore and spit it forward. Belts carry items. Inserters move items between belts and machines. Furnaces and Miners burn coal as fuel.
  • Research (T): build Labs, feed them science packs, unlock better toys.
  • Win: research Rocketry, build a Silo, feed it 10 Rocket Parts, hit LAUNCH.

CONTROLS

  • WASD/arrows pan · scroll zoom · middle-drag pan
  • 1–9 pick a building · R rotate · Q/Esc cancel
  • Left-click / drag place · Right-click / drag demolish (refunds everything)
  • Click any machine to open it. Click its slots to take items out; use the buttons to put fuel/ingredients in.
  • E/C crafting & inventory · T research · H this screen

TIPS

  • Miners output to the tile they face — point them at a belt, chest, or straight into a furnace.
  • Furnaces and assemblers only load/unload through inserters (or your own two hands).
  • Inserters grab from the tile behind them and drop to the tile in front.
  • Inserters can feed coal into miners and furnaces. Automate fuel early — hand-feeding gets old.
  • Ore patches are finite. The map is infinite. Expand.
